Quick examples

These are simple scenarios to help users answer confusing inputs correctly.

US employee moving back

Indian citizen. Mainly lived abroad. Return year often fits Citizen / PIO visiting India. Working in the US on W-2 before return usually means not liable elsewhere = No.

Dubai no-tax case

Indian citizen with India-source income above ₹15 lakh and no tax liability in any other country can trigger the deemed resident check. These cases are usually RNOR, not ROR.

Frequent India visitor

Citizen or PIO visiting India with India-source income above ₹15 lakh can face the 120-day rule instead of the usual visit threshold.

Tax rules can change and edge cases exist. This page is for planning and education only.
